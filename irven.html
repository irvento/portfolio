ARGOMsqrd

ARGOMsqrd is a comprehensive GIS and AR-enabled Digital Mapping and Obituary Management System I led as the lead developer during my 4th-year capstone project. The system was designed to modernize operations at the Manolo Fortich Memorial Park, tackling inefficiency, congestion, and manual record-keeping prevalent in public cemeteries.

Developed using an Agile-Scrum framework, the web application features interactive grave mapping via OpenStreetMap, AR-guided navigation for visitors, a digital obituary platform, and genealogy visualization. Its successful evaluation—scoring an excellent 77.33 on the System Usability Scale (SUS) and high marks for user acceptance—demonstrates its effectiveness in improving operational efficiency and visitor experience, aligning with Sustainable Development Goals for smart community infrastructure.

HRStreamline

HRStreamline is a full-stack Human Resources Information System (HRIS) designed to centralize and automate core HR functions. It serves as a secure, unified repository for all employee data—from personal details and employment history to compensation, performance reviews, and benefits.

By automating routine administrative tasks, the system significantly reduces manual errors, increases operational efficiency, and enables data-driven decision-making. HRStreamline empowers organizations to align human capital management with strategic mission and vision, providing HR professionals with the tools to focus on development, engagement, and delivering a superior employee experience.

Null Crime

This project was created during the Emerging Technologies subject. Null Crime is a community-focused crime reporting and prevention platform developed to support SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. The system enables real-time crime reporting with photo/video uploads, interactive geographical crime mapping, and priority-based reporting features. It also provides public crime analytics and an educational safety module to raise awareness and promote prevention.

Designed to increase transparency and collaboration between citizens and law enforcement, the platform aims to create safer, more informed communities through accessible technology and data-driven insights.
